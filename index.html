About me

Design really comes to life when it’s built and experienced, not just drawn.

With a strong architectural foundation, my work focuses on turning ideas into clear, buildable interior and spatial solutions. I’m especially interested in space planning, material coordination, and producing AutoCAD drawings that actually support what happens on site—particularly in fast-paced environments like exhibitions and events.

My experience includes hospitality and furniture projects, along with hands-on exposure to interior finishing. This has shaped a practical way of thinking, where design decisions are always connected to how things will be executed in reality.
